About Me

I am an enthusiastic and highly driven Electronics and Computer Engineering student currently pursuing my studies at the Malawi University of Business and Applied Sciences (MUBAS). My academic journey is fueled by a profound passion for the rapidly evolving fields of Artificial Intelligence and data analysis, complemented by a strong affinity for powerful design tools such as Figma and MATLAB. I find immense satisfaction in bridging the gap between the technical and creative realms of engineering. This means I'm equally adept at delving into the intricacies of embedded systems and optimizing hardware performance as I am at crafting intuitive frontend designs, exploring innovative user interfaces, and building robust analytic workflows.


My academic and personal journey has been profoundly shaped by an innate love for problem-solving and a persistent desire to engineer impactful solutions that address real-world challenges. I thrive in collaborative environments, valuing the synergy that emerges when diverse minds unite.
