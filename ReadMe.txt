Chikondi Chambamba's Portfolio
A personal portfolio website showcasing my skills in frontend development and UI/UX design. The site serves as a central hub for my projects, professional background, and contact information.

Features
Responsive Design: The layout is optimized to look great on various screen sizes, from mobile phones to desktop monitors.

Hero Section: A captivating introduction to my work, including a brief summary of my skills and a call to action.

About Me Section: A detailed overview of my background as an Electronics and Computer Engineering student, my passions in AI and data analysis, and my collaborative experiences.

Projects Section: A showcase of my key projects with links to live designs on Figma, complete with descriptions and visuals.

Contact Section: Provides multiple ways to get in touch, including email, phone, and social media links.

Resume Link: A direct link to my resume.

Technologies Used
HTML5: For the core structure and semantic content of the website.

CSS3: For all styling, layout, and visual enhancements.

Font Awesome: For the social media icons and other visual elements.

Figma: The tool used to design the UI/UX for the showcased projects.
